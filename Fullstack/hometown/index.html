Top 3 activities to do at Vijayawada

Visit the Kanaka Durga Temple

Perched on Indrakeeladri Hill, this powerful temple dedicated to Goddess Durga is a major spiritual draw, offering stunning views of the Krishna River and city.

Explore Undavalli Caves

Discover ancient rock-cut caves with impressive sculptures, a testament to early Indian architecture, set amidst lush greenery.

Experience Bhavani Island & Prakasam Barrage

Take a boat to Bhavani Island, India's largest river island, for water sports (jet skiing, banana boats) and relaxation, then enjoy the engineering marvel of the Prakasam Barrage.
